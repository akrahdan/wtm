Our History

Desiring God began inauspiciously in the spring of 1994 when John Piper, then the 48-year-old pastor of Bethlehem Baptist Church, handed off the church’s tape ministry to his assistant Jon Bloom. Piper and Bloom founded the ministry and named it Desiring God after Piper's book Desiring God: Meditations of a Christian Hedonist.

Desiring God

Meditations of a Christian Hedonist

John Piper
The world sells a sinful, cheap joy at the buyers expense. God dispenses fullness of joy at the expense of his own Son, so that you may be eternally happy in him.

What started with tapes of sermons, a few of Piper’s first books, and a single annual event, in God’s timing, blossomed into a web ministry with more than 10,000 resources distributed worldwide.

Our Strategy

Today the ministry of Desiring God is focused on distributing content freely and widely via the web and producing more than 1,000 new resources each year, including:

  • Articles
  • Sermons & Messages
  • Books
  • Ask Pastor John
  • Look at the Book

These resources cover a range of topics from marriage and dating to prayer and the Bible to suffering and sexual purity and suffering — all for your joy in Jesus.

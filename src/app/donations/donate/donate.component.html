White Throne Ministries is a nonprofit Christian organization that does not depend on any parent organization for financial support. This ministry is sustained by your prayers and financial giving. If you've been blessed by any of our outreach programmes, you may consider being a monthly financial partner or provide a one - time gift donation for our ministry. Kindly also check out our ministry needs and prayerfully consider how the Lord can use you to finish His work by partnering with us.

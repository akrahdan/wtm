About Us

White Throne Ministries is a registered non - profit Christian organization based in Ghana, West Africa. Our name was carefully chosen from the Great White Throne Judgement scene found in Revelation 20:11-12. At White Throne Ministries, we believe that Christ actually begun the first phase of the judgement in the most holy place of the heavenly sanctuary way back in 1844. We believe that by the time Christ appears the second time every case would have been decided for life or death; and our Lord will be ready with His reward to give unto everyone according as his work shall be (Revelation 22:12). We therefore find it our solemn duty to warn everyone to order their lives in conformity to this solemn time of judgement, while there is still the opportunity to do so. Our name inspires us to work with speed and also seize opportunities to present the Present Truth of Revelation 14:6-12 to the world.

We Believe

We believe in all the fundamental doctrines of the Seventh-day Adventist Church. We know by experience that these doctrines are Bible – based, reasonable and beneficial to a person’s mental, physical and spiritual wellbeing.

The Team

Samuel Nyarko

Samuel Nyarko is the President / Speaker of White Throne Ministries.
